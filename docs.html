Getting Started

Welcome to the QR Menu Service documentation. This guide will help you set up and customize your digital menu system.

Creating Your Account

To get started, you'll need to create an account by filling out the registration form on our homepage. Once registered, you'll have access to your dashboard where you can manage your menus.

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides access to all the tools you need to manage your restaurant's digital menu. From here you can:

  • Create and edit menu items
  • Customize the appearance of your QR menu
  • View analytics and customer engagement
  • Manage multiple restaurant locations

Creating Your Menu

Follow these steps to create your first digital menu:

Step 1: Create Categories

Organize your menu into logical categories such as Appetizers, Main Courses, Desserts, etc.

Step 2: Add Menu Items

For each category, add your menu items with descriptions and prices.

Step 3: Generate QR Code

Once your menu is complete, generate a unique QR code that customers can scan to access your menu.

Customizing Appearance

Personalize your QR menu to match your restaurant's brand:

Color Schemes

Choose from pre-designed color schemes or create your own custom color palette.

Typography

Select fonts that reflect your restaurant's style and personality.

Layout Options

Choose from different layout options to best display your menu items.
